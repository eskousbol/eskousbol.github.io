Emily: The Person

Alright, I've spent a lot of time talking about what I do for school what activities I do, but I would like to take this opportunity to say a little bit about myself and what I do for fun that does not have hours recorded.

I would say, if I had to describe myself quickly, that I am honest, loyal, and kind to a fault. I like helping people, and I like using the skills I have developed to help people.

Now, when I'm not solving calculus problems or programming, I really like to bake. And I'm pretty good at it, too. Cookies, cakes, pastries of all kinds. It is a practice with a wonderful result, and to share with those around me is one of the best parts. I like painting with watercolours. I really enjoy listening to comedy podcasts, they make my long commute to the university substantially better. And so, while I am passionate about the mathematical sciences, there are many other things I am interested in, and each one enriches my life in different ways.
